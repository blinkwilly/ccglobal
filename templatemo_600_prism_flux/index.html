About Christ Central Global

Christ Central Global is a community centered on Jesus, committed to spreading the Gospel, discipling believers, and transforming lives by the power of God's Word and Spirit. We gather to worship, grow, and serve our cities and nations.

Our Mission

To reveal Christ and raise Kingdom-minded disciples who carry God's presence to every sphere of influence.

Our Vision

A global family of believers, passionately advancing the Gospel, building strong local churches, and impacting the nations.

Core Values

Love, Integrity, Excellence, Prayer, and Service. We reflect Christ in character and power, honoring God and people in all we do.
